Tejaswini Bobba Acts and Play


A Doll's House Ibsen

NURSE. At last I have found the box with the fancy dress. NORA. Thanks; put it on the table. NURSE. _[doing so]_. But it is very much in want of mending. NORA. I should like to tear it into a hundred thousand pieces. NURSE. What an idea! It can easily be put in order—just a little patience. NORA. Yes, I will go and get Mrs Linde to come and help me with it. NURSE. What, out again? In this horrible weather? You will catch cold, ma’am, and make yourself ill.
